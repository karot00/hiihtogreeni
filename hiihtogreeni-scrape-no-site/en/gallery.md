# Photo Gallery (English)

## Cabin in Levi

### Check it out, fall in love with it and book your perfect vacation in Lapland!

Hiihtogreeni is a cabin in Levi with two 12-person apartments. This high-quality duplex has all that corporate groups and other groups need. The icing on the cake of your stay will be the gorgeous fell, golf course and lake view in a perfect location.
